How JODO Works

1️⃣
👥

Community Formation

Each JODO Group covers a 50–100 meter radius — about 100 nearby families. Each contributes ₹100, creating a ₹10,000 local working capital. 👉 A self-funded, trust-based community economy.

2️⃣
🧑‍💼

Community Leader

A trusted resident becomes the Community Leader — managing orders, communication, and pickups. 💰 Earns ₹15K–₹20K/month by growing the group’s active families.

3️⃣
📱

Weekly Ordering

Families place orders via App or WhatsApp. Orders are aggregated and finalized 48 hours in advance for zero-waste procurement.

4️⃣
🌾

Direct Farmer Connection

JODO connects directly with farmers and pays them 100% within 24 hours of harvest. Ensuring fair prices and faster cash flow to the roots of India’s food chain.

5️⃣
🚚

Bulk Delivery

A single bulk load (~500 kg) is delivered to the community’s pickup point, cutting last-mile delivery costs and reducing waste.

6️⃣
🛒

Family Pickup & Refill

Each family walks 50–100 meters to collect their order, pay locally, and refill the working capital — keeping the JODO cycle continuously revolving.
